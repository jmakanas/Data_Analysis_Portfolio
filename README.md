# About

Hello, I'm James Makanas, and I'm excited to share my journey of transformation from the world of finance to the captivating realm of data analytics. Finance was the field I had originally chosen, but as I moved forward, I realized I had a natural inclination towards data-driven decision making, and thus, the evolving dynamics of our data-driven world have led me to make a bold career switch.

The decision to pivot towards data analytics is a testament to my unwavering commitment to personal growth and my recognition of the paramount importance of data in today's information-rich landscape. In this narrative, I'll walk you through the reasons behind my transition, the challenges I've encountered, and the exciting opportunities that await as I embark on this new chapter. Join me on this adventure of reinvention as I explore the world of data analytics, where numbers take on a whole new dimension and possibilities are limitless.
